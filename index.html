What is a Telegram bot?





A bot is just a simple Telegram account, which is controlled by software, not by people. It has a function of artificial intelligence (AI) and can do everything - teach, play, broadcast, remind and even interact with other services.

What it can do?


Give you info

The user needs to formulate a request and then bot can easily give you necessary information. Sometimes the bot also offers answer options to filter out useless info

News, jokes, links and articles are available to everyone; everything you need - a mobile phone and a free Telegram account.

Interact with other services

It can help you to choose a movie, to order a meal, to call a taxi, and even to pay bills.

Hold interactive games

Quiz with other users in a public chat or a «Hangman» game are now available in one application.

What about advantages?

Why should I use Telegram bot instead of a mobile application?

Cheap

As you know, it is very difficult and expensive to create and support applications for every existing platform. Bot provides you with a simple, quick and cheap way to create your own service for any goal.

Lightweight

There is no profit in downloading dozens of separate applications that occupy a huge amount of space and memory when it is possible just to download the free Telegram application and use the same features. “All in one place” - an obvious advantage.

Convenient

Due to the elements of artificial intelligence bot can easily understand natural language queries and respond to them in the same human language.
